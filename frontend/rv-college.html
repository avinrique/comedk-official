About RV College of Engineering

RV College of Engineering (RVCE) is one of India's premier engineering institutions, established in 1963 in the heart of Bangalore. Spread across a lush 52-acre campus, RVCE is affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU) and holds autonomous status, allowing it to design its own curriculum and examination system.

Accredited with NAAC A+ grade and NBA accreditation for multiple programs, RVCE consistently ranks among the top 15 engineering colleges in India. The college is known for its excellent academic standards, world-class infrastructure, and an outstanding placement record that attracts top recruiters from around the globe.

With over 14 undergraduate programs, state-of-the-art labs, strong industry connections, and a vibrant campus life, RVCE provides a holistic educational experience that prepares students for successful careers in engineering and technology.

Admission Process

Admission Quotas

  • KCET Quota (45%): For Karnataka domicile students through Karnataka CET counselling. Lowest fee structure among all quotas.
  • COMEDK Quota (30%): Open to all-India students through COMEDK UGET counselling. Moderate fee structure.
  • Management Quota (25%): Direct admission through the college with higher fees. Students must still have appeared for KCET/COMEDK/JEE Main.

How to Apply

  • Appear for COMEDK UGET / KCET / JEE Main entrance exam
  • Participate in the respective counselling process
  • Choose RVCE and preferred branch based on your rank
  • Complete document verification and fee payment

Eligibility & Entrance Exams

B.E. Programs

  • Must have passed 10+2 / 2nd PUC with Physics, Mathematics, and Chemistry/Biotechnology/Computer Science
  • Minimum 45% aggregate for General category (40% for SC/ST/OBC)
  • Valid KCET / COMEDK UGET / JEE Main score

Accepted Entrance Exams

  • KCET — Karnataka Common Entrance Test (Karnataka students only)
  • COMEDK UGET — Open to all-India students
  • JEE Main — Accepted for management quota consideration
  • GATE — For M.Tech admissions

Frequently Asked Questions

The COMEDK cutoff for CSE at RVCE typically falls within the top 800-1500 ranks. However, cutoffs vary each year based on the number of applicants and seat availability. Use our LS Consultancy Services tool with your COMEDK score for a personalized estimate.
The COMEDK quota annual fee for all B.E. programs at RVCE is approximately Rs. 3.3 lakh per year, totaling around Rs. 13.2 lakh for the full 4-year program. This is uniform across all branches.
The highest placement package at RVCE is approximately Rs. 67 LPA. The average package for B.E. students ranges from Rs. 6-8 LPA overall, with top branches like CSE averaging Rs. 15-16 LPA.
RVCE accepts KCET (for Karnataka students), COMEDK UGET (all-India), and JEE Main (for management quota). For M.Tech, GATE scores are required. For MBA/MCA, Karnataka PGCET or KMAT are accepted.
RVCE is consistently ranked #1 among private engineering colleges in Karnataka. With NAAC A+ accreditation, autonomous status, a 52-acre campus, and placement records featuring companies like Google and Microsoft, it is considered one of the best choices for engineering in South India.
