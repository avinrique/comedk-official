Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Learn how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Last updated: 15 April 2026

LS Consultancy Services ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and disclose information when you use our website at lspredictor.com (the "Website") and our education consultancy services (the "Services").

By using our Website and Services, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

a) Information You Provide Directly

  • Personal identification: Full name, email address, and phone number when you fill out contact forms, register for consultancy services, or use the lead gate on our predictor tool.
  • Exam and academic data: Exam scores, ranks, category, preferred branches, and other academic details that you enter into our college predictor tool.
  • Communication data: Messages, queries, and feedback you send to us through email, phone, WhatsApp, or the contact form.

b) Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage data: Pages visited, time spent on pages, features used, and interactions with the predictor tool.
  • Device and browser information: IP address, browser type and version, operating system, device type, and screen resolution.
  • Referral data: The website or source that referred you to our Website.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Service delivery: To provide college predictions based on the exam data you enter and to deliver personalised admission guidance.
  • Counselling follow-up: To contact you regarding your admission queries, offer consultancy services, and provide updates relevant to your educational interests.
  • Service improvement: To analyse usage patterns, improve the accuracy of our predictor algorithms, and enhance the overall user experience.
  • Communication: To respond to your enquiries, send appointment reminders, and provide important updates about our services.
  • Legal compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes.

3. Information Sharing

We value your trust and are committed to responsible data handling:

  • We do not sell your personal data. Your information is never sold, rented, or traded to third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.
  • Service providers: We may share limited information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating the Website and delivering our services (such as hosting providers and analytics tools). These providers are contractually obligated to keep your data confidential and use it only for the purposes we specify.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government regulation, or if we believe disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, the safety of our users, or the public.
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of the transaction. We will notify you of any such change.

4. Cookies

Our Website may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the Website to function properly, including session management and security.
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our Website by collecting anonymised usage data (such as pages visited and time on site).
  • Preference cookies: Remember your settings and preferences (such as exam selections) to provide a more personalised experience.

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of the Website.

5. Data Security

We take appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Secure transmission of data using SSL/TLS encryption.
  • Restricted access to personal data, limited to authorised personnel who need it to perform their duties.
  • Regular review of our data collection, storage, and processing practices.
  • Password hashing and secure authentication mechanisms for our internal systems.

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we are committed to maintaining industry-standard protections.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by law. Specifically:

  • Predictor usage data (exam scores entered) may be retained for up to 24 months for service improvement purposes.
  • Contact and consultancy records are retained for the duration of the client relationship and for a reasonable period thereafter for legal and business purposes.
  • You may request deletion of your data at any time (see "Your Rights" below).

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: You may request that we correct any inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • Deletion: You may request that we delete your personal data, subject to any legal obligations that require us to retain it.
  • Objection: You may object to the processing of your personal data for certain purposes, such as marketing communications.
  • Withdraw consent: Where processing is based on your consent, you may withdraw that consent at any time by contacting us.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details provided below. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Services are intended for students and parents involved in the college admission process. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 without parental consent. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Your continued use of the Website after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
