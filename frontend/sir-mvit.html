About Sir MVIT

Sir M. Visvesvaraya Institute of Technology (Sir MVIT) was established in 1986 by the Sri Krishnadevaraya Educational Trust. Located on a sprawling 45+ acre campus in Hunasamaranahalli near Bangalore International Airport, Sir MVIT is named after the legendary engineer and Bharat Ratna recipient Sir M. Visvesvaraya.

Affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU) and accredited with NAAC A grade, Sir MVIT offers a comprehensive range of engineering programs. The college is known for its serene campus, excellent infrastructure, and commitment to producing competent engineers.

The institution provides modern laboratories, a central library, innovation center, and excellent hostel facilities. Sir MVIT emphasizes holistic development through cultural activities, sports, technical clubs, and community service initiatives.

Placements at Sir MVIT

  • Highest package of 38 LPA with average packages ranging from 5-7 LPA
  • Over 80% placement rate for eligible students
  • Top recruiters include Wipro, Infosys, TCS, Cognizant, Accenture, HCL, and Mindtree
  • Regular campus recruitment drives with IT and core engineering companies
  • Pre-placement training programs including technical skills and soft skills development
