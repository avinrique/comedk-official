About REVA University

REVA University, established in 2012, is a private university located on a scenic 45-acre campus in Yelahanka, Bangalore. As a university with UGC recognition and NAAC A+ accreditation, REVA has the advantage of designing its own curriculum and awarding its own degrees.

REVA offers 13+ B.Tech programs with specializations in emerging fields like AI&ML, IoT, Data Science, and Robotics. The university also accepts multiple entrance exams including REVA Entrance, KCET, COMEDK, and JEE Main, providing flexible admission pathways for students.

With a modern campus featuring state-of-the-art labs, sports facilities, and hostel accommodation, REVA provides a complete university experience for students from across India.

Admission Process

  • REVA Entrance Exam: University's own entrance test
  • KCET / COMEDK / JEE Main: Also accepted for admission
  • Direct Admission: Based on 12th marks and entrance scores

Eligibility & Entrance Exams

  • 10+2 with PCM, minimum 45% aggregate
  • Valid REVA Entrance / KCET / COMEDK / JEE Main score

Frequently Asked Questions

REVA is a private university recognized by UGC. It designs its own curriculum and awards its own degrees, unlike VTU-affiliated colleges.
