About Us

About LS Predictor

We are on a mission to simplify the college admission process for every student in India through data-driven predictions and expert guidance.

Our Mission

To empower every student with accurate, accessible, and data-driven college admission predictions. We believe that choosing the right college should not be based on guesswork or expensive consultants. By leveraging technology and comprehensive data analysis, we aim to level the playing field so that students from all backgrounds can make informed decisions about their academic future.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted platform for college admission guidance. We envision a future where every student, regardless of their location or financial situation, has access to the same quality of admission counselling that was previously available only to a few. We are building the tools that will shape how millions of students approach one of the most important decisions of their lives.

Our Story

How It All Started

LS Predictor was born from a personal frustration. When our founders went through the college admission process, they spent weeks researching cutoff trends, comparing colleges, and trying to figure out where they realistically had a chance of getting in. The information was scattered across dozens of websites, often outdated, and sometimes unreliable.

We realized that thousands of students face this same struggle every year. The data exists, but it is not presented in a way that is easy to understand and act upon. That is when we decided to build a platform that brings all this information together and uses intelligent algorithms to provide clear, actionable predictions.

Starting with JEE Main predictions in 2023, we have since expanded to cover NEET, COMEDK, SRM, and VIT. Today, over 50,000 students have used LS Predictor to make smarter admission decisions, and we continue to grow our database and improve our algorithms every admission cycle.
