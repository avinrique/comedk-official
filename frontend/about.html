About Us

About LS Consultancy Services

A registered education consultancy in Bangalore helping students navigate engineering college admissions in Karnataka since 2017.

Who We Are

LS Consultancy Services is a registered education consultancy based in Bangalore, established in 2017. We help students navigate engineering college admissions in Karnataka through COMEDK, KCET, and JEE counselling. Our goal is to provide clear, honest guidance so students and parents can make informed decisions about college admissions without confusion or misinformation.

What We Do

We assist students through the entire admission process — from understanding exam results and cutoff trends to selecting the right college and branch. Whether you are appearing for COMEDK, KCET, or JEE, we provide personalised counselling to help you make the best choice based on your rank, preferences, and budget.

Our Story

How We Got Started

LS Consultancy Services was founded by Ravi Prakash in 2017 as an offline consultancy in Bangalore, initially focused on helping students with COMEDK admissions. Having seen how confusing the counselling process can be for students and parents, Ravi started the consultancy to provide straightforward, reliable guidance.

Over the years, we expanded our services to cover KCET, JEE Main, and other entrance exams. As more students reached out from across Karnataka, we recognised the need for an online tool that could give quick, data-driven college predictions based on actual cutoff data.

That led us to build our online rank predictor tool, which uses historical cutoff data to help students understand their chances at various colleges. While the tool provides a useful starting point, we continue to offer personal counselling for students who need more detailed, one-on-one guidance through the admission process.
